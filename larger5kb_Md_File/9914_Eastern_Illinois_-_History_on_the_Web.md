This is a basic gateway to useful and, generally, reliable history sites.
These sites have been selected and organized by Newton Key, with suggestions
from Nora Pat Small, David Smith, Ben Fallaw, and Chris Waldrep. We seek to
list reliable sites (with documentation) of interest to professional
historians and students of history. We invite [corrections and
suggestions](mailto:histweb@eiu.edu). Eastern Illinois University
