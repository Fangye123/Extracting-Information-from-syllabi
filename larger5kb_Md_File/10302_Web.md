[http://www.lib.duke.edu/forest/biblio.html](http://www.lib.duke.edu/forest/biblio.html)

For over 50 years the Society has monitored the world of publishing for items
written as history and dealing with the utilization, management, and
appreciation of our forest related resources. Since the early 1980s the
information has been maintained in a computer database and is continually
updated. As of early 1999 the bibliography contained over 25,000 annotated
citations to books, articles and dissertations providing a historical
perspective on topics. (Note by FHS)
