![](../images/transparent_square.gif) | ECE 8020 Professional Communications
Skills came into being in response to industry's request for improved
communication skills from graduates of ECE. The course was designed through
continual consultation between senior ECE faculty and the Graduate
Professional Communications lecturer. As evidence of the School's commitment
to improving the communication skills of students, ECE 8020 is the only
required course for a Ph.D. in the School of Electrical and Computer
Engineering.
